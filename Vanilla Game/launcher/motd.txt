Welcome to Crusader Kings II version 2.4! This update expands the map deep into Mongolia and the Tarim Basin, and also includes a huge amount of general improvements to the game, including improvements in performance and mod support.

Be sure to check out the new expansion - Crusader Kings II: Horse Lords - which allows you to play as a nomad ruler, giving you access to a range of new mechanics and gameplay. Other features include the Silk Road and new mercenaries and adventurers.
